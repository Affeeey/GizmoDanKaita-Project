About Gizmodankaita Office Plus

Learn about our mission, vision, and the values that drive us

Our Story

Gizmodankaita Office Plus was founded with a vision to bridge the gap between technology education and entrepreneurship in our community. We bring together diverse talents and expertise to create a holistic approach to development.

Our team consists of professionals from various fields including computer engineering, software development, and business management, all united by a common goal: to create a society where everyone contributes to its development.

Since our inception, we have impacted hundreds of students through our tech workshops and helped numerous entrepreneurs turn their ideas into viable businesses.

Our Mission

To create a society where everyone contributes to its development through technology education, entrepreneurship support, and community engagement.

We achieve this by:

  • Providing quality tech education and training
  • Supporting entrepreneurs with consultancy services
  • Organizing workshops in tech schools
  • Fostering community development initiatives
  • Promoting innovation and creativity

Our Vision

To be the leading catalyst for tech education and entrepreneurial success in our community, fostering innovation and sustainable development.

We envision:

  • A community where technology drives development
  • Entrepreneurs empowered to create sustainable businesses
  • Youth equipped with relevant tech skills for the future
  • Collaborative partnerships that amplify our impact
  • A self-sustaining ecosystem of innovation

Our Values

Collaboration

We believe in the power of working together - different people coming together as one to achieve common goals.

Education

We are committed to empowering individuals through knowledge sharing and skill development.

Sustainability

We focus on creating lasting impact through sustainable solutions and community-driven development.
