Strategic Partnerships

We believe in the power of collaboration. Our partnerships with educational institutions and organizations help us extend our reach and impact in the community.

Katsina State Institute of Technology and Management

We collaborate with KSITM on various tech education initiatives, including workshops, training programs, and knowledge exchange activities that benefit both students and faculty.

Collaboration Areas: Tech workshops, Student mentorship, Curriculum development

Umaru Musa Yar'adua University

Our partnership with UMYU focuses on entrepreneurship development and research collaborations. We work together to foster innovation and support student-led startups.

Collaboration Areas: Entrepreneurship programs, Research projects, Startup incubation

Alqalam University Katsina

We partner with Alqalam University to promote tech education and digital literacy. Our joint initiatives include coding bootcamps, tech awareness programs, and skill development workshops.

Collaboration Areas: Digital literacy, Coding bootcamps, Tech awareness programs

Gafai Communications

Our partnership with Gafai Communications focuses on IT infrastructure development and digital solutions for businesses and educational institutions in the region.

Collaboration Areas: IT solutions, Infrastructure development, Digital transformation
